ORANGE

我心里有一簇迎着烈日而生的花， 比一切美酒都要芬芳， 滚烫的馨香淹没过稻草人的胸膛， 草扎的精神，从此万寿无疆。——Priest《默读》

My chest roots a cluster of flowers hugging the scorching sun, floral stronger than any wines in the world. The boiling fragrance floods me. I am a jackstraw. Even though the dead grass riddled my soul. (Priest, "The light in the night")
